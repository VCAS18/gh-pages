About

VCAS - “The International Conference on VLSI, Communication and Signal Processing”. The conference is proposed for three days tentitively from Nov. 29, 2018 to Dec. 1, 2018.

This conference will provide plateform for persons working in academia and industry, as well as students to get recognition for their research innovations and the Institute will also get a wide publicity. In general, the scope of the conference is Electronics and Communication Engineering and will involve all class of students having research orientation.

The conference will particularly focus on the emerging areas in the field of VLSI, Communication and Signal Processing.

For the technical program it is proposed to have several keynote speakers from academic institutes and industry, and sessions involving research papers presentation, tutorial, workshops etc. The papers submitted shall undergo a standard review process for acceptance.
